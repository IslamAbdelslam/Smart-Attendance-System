About

About the Project

A graduation project combining Computer Vision and Deep Learning to solve a real problem in academic institutions.

🎯

The Problem

Manual attendance in university classrooms is time-consuming, error-prone, and disruptive to lectures. For large classes, it can take up to 10 minutes per session and leads to inaccurate records.

💡

Our Solution

A camera placed at the classroom entrance captures a single frame as students walk in. The AI pipeline detects, embeds, and matches every face in milliseconds. Attendance is logged automatically with zero human intervention.

📈

Results

Training done on students images fro MAnsoura University in diverse in lighting, positions , and different movements
